# Report Pages

## 1. Executive Overview

Purpose: Give leadership a fast view of fleet health by company.

Use:

- KPI cards for total miles, active vehicles, overdue vehicles, speeding events
- clustered bar chart for miles by company
- clustered bar chart for overdue vehicles by company
- line chart for speeding events over time
- slicers for company and date range

## 2. Mileage Tracking

Purpose: Show utilization and identify the vehicles driving the most miles.

Use:

- line chart for total miles by date
- bar chart for top vehicles by miles driven
- matrix for company, vehicle, and total miles
- table with vehicle, company, current odometer, average speed, trip count

## 3. Overdue Service

Purpose: Highlight maintenance risk.

Use:

- KPI cards for overdue by date and overdue by mileage
- table with vehicle, company, service type, next due date, next due mileage, current odometer, service status label
- bar chart for overdue vehicles by company
- donut chart for service status label

## 4. Speeding Report

Purpose: Surface safety issues and repeat offenders.

Use:

- KPI cards for total speeding events, severe speeding events, average over speed limit
- bar chart for speeding events by company
- bar chart for speeding events by vehicle
- bar chart for speeding events by driver
- map using latitude and longitude

## 5. Company Comparison

Purpose: Benchmark companies against each other fairly.

Use:

- matrix with company, total miles, overdue vehicles, speeding events, events per 1K miles, service cost per mile
- scatter chart for total miles vs speeding events per 1K miles
- scatter chart for total miles vs service cost per mile
- conditional formatting to flag poor performers
