    About ChefTable

    A marketplace for in-home dining experiences.

    ChefTable helps private chefs present a polished menu, highlight their culinary style, and accept booking requests from guests who want a memorable at-home meal.

    For chefs

    Showcase signature dishes, set expectations, and collect qualified inquiries in one place.

    For guests

    Browse chefs, compare menu styles, and request a date without a long back-and-forth.

    For events

    Perfect for dinner parties, anniversaries, brunches, tastings, and celebration dinners at home.

    Built on a simple stack

    Application ChefTable
    Framework Flask
    Interface Tabler + custom marketplace styling
    Core experience Chef profiles, menus, booking requests, and a lightweight Chef Studio inbox
