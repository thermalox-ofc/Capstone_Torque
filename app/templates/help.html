    How to use ChefTable

    Whether you are booking a chef or showcasing your own menu, the core flow is intentionally simple.

    For guests

    Browse the chef directory to compare cuisines, pricing, and service areas.
    Open a chef profile to review their menu and send your date, guest count, location, and occasion.
    After submitting, use the confirmation page details while you wait for the chef to follow up.

    For chefs

    Use Chef Studio to review incoming booking requests and event details.
    Keep your menu clear and specific so guests understand your style, specialties, and pricing range.
    Reply quickly to new inquiries to improve conversion and create a premium experience from the first touchpoint.
